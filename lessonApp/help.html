Menu
Class
Before you started, you must login into the apps rst. You can create the classes that you teach and then click button View Class to see your classes.

Objective / Planning
If you click at the class that you have created on the list of class, it will go to the create objective page and you can choose your week . After that, you can key in the Topic, Objective, Duration, Activity, Materialand step of your activity.Click create objective button to save your planning.You can view all of your objective when you click at the View Objetive button.

Timetable
You can snap your timetable picture just click on the snap button at the right top of the timetable page and save it directly into your mobile phone.

Notify
you can create your important task that has been planned, just click the notify button and you can insert the data that you want to Remind for you.
